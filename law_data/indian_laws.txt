Section 302 IPC - Punishment for Murder: Whoever commits murder shall be punished with death, or imprisonment for life, and shall also be liable to fine.

Section 420 IPC - Cheating and dishonestly inducing delivery of property: Whoever cheats and thereby dishonestly induces the person deceived to deliver any property ... shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to seven years, and shall also be liable to fine.

Section 376 IPC - Punishment for Rape: A man shall be punished for committing rape with rigorous imprisonment of not less than ten years, but which may extend to life imprisonment ...
